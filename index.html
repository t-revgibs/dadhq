Katy-Area Go-To Spots

🎯 Dad Strategy: Always-Free Spots You Can Do Any Weekend

These spots are low/no cost and perfect for active, hands-on kids. Bookmark them all — they're your backup plan when you need something on short notice.

📍 KATY PRIORITY SPOTS

🎭 Katy Play Station

Katy, TX · FREE
Railroad-inspired inclusive play area. Great for active play — both boys can run wild here. Free admission always.
FreeActiveKaty

🐟 Mary Jo Peckham Park

Katy · FREE
Stocked fishing pond, walking trails, expandable playground, and FREE putt-putt golf. Bring a rod for the 10-year-old and let the 6-year-old go wild on the playground. Perfect dad day.
FreeActiveKaty

📚 Katy Branch Library (Harris County)

5414 Franz Rd, Katy · 281-391-3509 · FREE
Recurring weekly programs for school-age kids: crafts, science, story time, STEAM take-home kits. Family Place Learning Center is great for interactive hands-on activities. Check hcpl.net weekly for schedule.
FreeScienceKaty

📚 Maud Marks Branch Library

1815 Westgreen Blvd, Katy · FREE
Harris County Public Library with dedicated Family Place Learning Center. Weekly programs for kids including crafts, reading challenges, and interactive learning. Second library option if Katy Branch is busy.
FreeLearningKaty

🛍️ LaCenterra at Cinco Ranch

Grand Pkwy & Cinco Ranch Blvd, Katy · Free to explore
34-acre outdoor shopping area with open common spaces. Farmers Market every Sunday at 11 AM. Great for a casual stroll, food, and outdoor time. Often has seasonal family events too.
FreeKaty

🏛️ Historic Downtown Katy

904 Avenue C, Katy · Usually Free
Katy Market Day every 3rd Saturday (March–Nov), seasonal festivals, the Railroad Museum & caboose. Great for exploring local heritage. Kids like the train history!
FreeKaty
🚗 NEARBY — 20–40 MIN DRIVE

⚾ Constellation Field (Space Cowboys)

One Stadium Dr, Sugar Land · ~20 min from Katy
The closest professional baseball stadium to you. Triple-A Astros affiliate. Cheap tickets, fireworks Fridays, dollar hot dog Wednesdays, and Sunday family packs. Perfect for both boys — action-packed and lots of room to move.
SportsActive

🚀 Space Center Houston

1601 NASA Pkwy, Houston · ~45 min · Paid admission
The ultimate science spot for your crew. Rockets, astronaut training simulators, hands-on STEM exhibits. Your 10-year-old will be mind-blown. Check for free admission days (often for Houston residents).
ScienceHands-On

🧪 Children's Museum Houston

1500 Binz St, Houston · FREE Thursdays 5–8 PM
Interactive science exhibits, building zones, water play, coding stations. Perfect for both ages — lots of physical activity AND brain engagement. FREE every Thursday evening. Worth the drive.
Free ThuScienceHands-On

🦕 Houston Museum of Natural Science

5555 Hermann Park Dr, Houston · Free Thursdays after 3 PM
Massive dinosaur hall, planetarium, gems, Egyptian mummies. Your 6-year-old will lose his mind at the T-Rex. Free on Thursday afternoons — combine with the Children's Museum for an epic day.
Free ThuScience

⚽ Shell Energy Stadium (Dynamo)

2200 Texas Ave, Houston · ~35 min
Houston Dynamo FC (MLS) and Houston Dash (NWSL) play here. Great outdoor stadium with loud supporter sections — kids love the vibe. Next home game: March 14 vs. Portland at 7:30 PM.
SportsActive

📚 Cinco Ranch Branch Library (Fort Bend)

2620 Commercial Center Blvd, Katy · Reopened March 9!
Fort Bend County Library branch — back open as of March 9. Kids programs, story time, STEAM activities. Check fortbendcountytx.gov/library for current schedule.
FreeKaty Area
🌿 Katy Parks — Always Free, Always Open

🎵 Central Green — LaCenterra

23501 Cinco Ranch Blvd, Katy · willowforkdrainagedistrict.com · FREE
Half-acre outdoor stage and lawn right inside LaCenterra. Free concerts, outdoor yoga (every Wednesday 8–10 AM), Tai Chi every Saturday, live music events throughout spring. Kids can run the open lawn while you catch live entertainment. Bring a blanket and chairs. Free parking at LaCenterra.
FreeActiveKaty
📅 Upcoming: Apr 4 — Nightbird live music · Apr 18 — Moodafaruka multicultural music · Every Wed: Yoga on the Green

🌳 Willow Fork Park

23440 Cinco Ranch Blvd, Katy · Open daylight hours · FREE
32-acre gem right across from LaCenterra. Paved trails around a scenic lake, disc golf course (great for you and the 10-year-old!), playground, butterfly garden, bird watching, pavilion with grills. Monarch butterfly waystation — certified by Monarch Watch. Bunnies, turtles in the lake, free WiFi, educational nature signs throughout. No large events allowed — it stays peaceful and accessible always.
FreeActiveNatureKaty
💡 Dad tip: Combine with Central Green — both are on Cinco Ranch Blvd, back to back. Full afternoon, zero dollars.

🌻 Fulshear — Eagle Landing Park (NEW!)

4131 Woods Road, Fulshear · ~15 min from Katy · FREE
Brand new $5.2 million park that just opened in November 2025 and was annexed into Fulshear city limits in January 2026. Walking trails, sports fields with public access (HTX Soccer has priority but 2 fields always reserved for public), playground. Phase 1 grand opening of fields is Spring 2026 — this will be a great soccer kick-around spot for the boys! Full park opens Summer 2027.
FreeActiveFulshear
📅 Follow fulsheartexas.gov for upcoming events. Fulshear's main annual events: Fulshear's Folly (October) and various spring community events.

🏙️ Brookshire — Hangar Unity Center

4010 4th St, Brookshire · ~20 min from Katy · FREE programs
Community center in Brookshire with free ongoing programs. Teen Hang Time Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri 2:30–6 PM (great for your 10-year-old — computer lab, lounge, pool, video games). Hip Hop Hope every 1st & 3rd Thursday with free food, music, and games. Check brookshiretexas.org for current schedule.
FreeBrookshire
